Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cook Time: 10 Minutes

Serves: 1 Serving

Level: Beginner

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • ½ avocado (mashed)
  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook in a pan with olive oil over medium heat, stirring gently.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on toasted whole wheat bread.
  4. Serve scrambled eggs on top or on the side.
