Lemon Garlic Salmon with Steamed Vegetables

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: 18 Minutes

Serves: 2 Serving

Level: Intermediate

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ lemon (juiced)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli and carrots

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Coat salmon with the mixture and bake for 15-18 minutes.
  4. Serve with steamed vegetables on the side.
