Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa & Chickpeas

Prep Time: 15 Minutes

Cook Time: 30 Minutes

Serves: 3 Servings

Level: Advanced

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 large bell peppers (cut tops off, remove seeds)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • ½ cup canned chickpeas (rinsed)
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup shredded cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix quinoa, chickpeas, tomatoes, spices, and olive oil.
  3. Stuff bell peppers with the mixture and top with cheese.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.
