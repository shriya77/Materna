Chicken & Vegetable Stew

Prep Time: 15 Minutes

Cook Time: 6 Hours (slow cooked)

Serves: 4 Servings

Level: Advanced

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast (cubed)
  • 1 small potato (diced)
  • 1 small carrot (sliced)
  • ½ cup green beans (chopped)
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan, brown chicken for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add onions and garlic, cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a slow cooker, add vegetables, broth, and thyme.
  4. Cook on low for 6 hours or until chicken is tender.
