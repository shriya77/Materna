Spinach & Cheese Omelet

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cook Time: 5 Minutes

Serves: 1 Serving

Level: Beginner

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup fresh spinach (chopped)
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté spinach for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour eggs over spinach, cook for 2 minutes, add cheese, and fold.
  4. Cook for another minute and serve warm.
