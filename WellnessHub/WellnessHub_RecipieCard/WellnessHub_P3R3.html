Lentil & Sweet Potato Soup

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: 25 Minutes

Serves: 2 Serving

Level: Intermediate

About the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 small sweet potato (diced)
  • 1 small carrot (chopped)
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pot, sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add sweet potato, carrot, and cumin, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add lentils and broth, bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Blend for a smooth texture or serve chunky.
